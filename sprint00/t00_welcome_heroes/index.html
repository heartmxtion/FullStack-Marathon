Superhero Trivia

What makes a superhero?

Superheroes are characters in comics, movies, and TV shows that possess extraordinary abilities and use them to fight evil and protect the innocent.
They often have distinctive costumes, secret identities, and a strong moral code.

My favorite superhero

Iron Man

Tony Stark created the Iron Man suit to save his own life after being critically injured. He later uses the suit to fight crime and protect the world from threats. Iron Man is known for his high-tech gadgets, witty one-liners, and close relationship with his assistant, Pepper Potts.
